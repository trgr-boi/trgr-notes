Trgr, Start

24/09/2025

Hello, this is Trgr.

I've always felt the need to share. I think everyone does.
I don't know if it was the lack of close friends or the fascination with the internet, but I always did this through an online outlet.

But it was never really something that was "myself". I never wanted to be known by strangers. This is a project I start to change this. To have an outlet where I show myself to the world. To learn how to put my thoughts in words, and to start reflecting on my thoughts and opinions before I internalise them.

I am very dark and pessimistic — even nihilistic at times — person, that tries not to drown in it. For a long time I hated myself and projected this onto the world. Now I don't hate myself anymore at all, but this negative view of the world remains. The disgust of how we, as a civilisation, treat others is still very active. In many forms. Towards many things and people.

Still, I don't want this to just be a place filled with hatred and nihilism.
I also want to share about the things I like and about the hope I have for the future.
But I cannot promise anything.

This is meant for people who want to kill time.
I cannot promise anything interesting. This is mainly written for myself, with an allowance of a third party. Not meant to be formal, but still thoughtful.

Don't expect anything regular. I don't expect anyone reading this.
So no one has any obligations.
