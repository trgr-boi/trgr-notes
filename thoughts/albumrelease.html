Released an album again

16/11/2025

Hello, trgr here

I finally released an album again. It isn’t one I made recently, but one that was just collecting dust on my sorage somewhere.
I still have to upload all my old music to Bandcamp. Not that I want to grow or anything, but just to have a public archive of what I made.

The album is called ‘Homeworld: 2073’. It’s an old jungle live set I did back in October of 2023. For those that want to know, the concept for the album (not the music) is greatly inspired by artists like Deepspace, who try to tell stories through titles, and descriptions. This I want to do more : )

The music itself is some classic jungle, now considered Intelligent DnB. Most of them consisting of classic jungle samples from packs like “Blu Mar Ten – Jungle Jungle” and “Zero-G Jungle Warfare 1, 2 & 3” sample packs. Sometimes so much that it was just drag and drop and a little bit of mixing.
But it was fun to make this!
