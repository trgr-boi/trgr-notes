February 2026

This month had gone a bit better. I’m still struggling with some of the same things, but my outlook on it has become much more stable. I am not hating myself as much and know that I can change for the better.

I have decided to stay sober for a while. Not that it was a massive issue before, but I find myself thinking about it regularly when I feel bad. So it is better to not give myself the option at all then to waste all my mental energy on debating myself. Though, this hasn't really fixed anything on it's own. I still go to bed too late, I still leave trash laying around, and I still procrastinate until I have barely enough time to finish what needs to be done.

During times like these, I always used to gravitate towards "productivity system prep". Yea, you know I didn't get shit done but I had a pretty cool setup... that never really got used. So I'm trying to keep it simple. 1 place I have to check in the morning, 1 place I have to check for school tasks,... Not a giant, interconnected system that I will forget how to use within a month. I want to just try and focus on what I need to do.

Taking care of myself has also gone better. The routine of cooking for myself has been starting to come back, as well as the routing of tidying up. I found that consciously cutting out screen-time has helped a lot. The "feeling" bored, that I almost never allow myself to do, drives me to then just clean up. What else should I do?

I still feel constantly on the edge of a breakdown, though. I get very easily annoyed and think that everything will become a fight. So deep down I know I'm not really doing well. But I'm still able to win against it. Hopefully I can keep this going.
